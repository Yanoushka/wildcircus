Once upon a time...(Photo : View of the Wild Circus in New York City)
The Wild Circus company has been established in 1856 by two brothers, Achille Zavetti and Pipo Zavetti.
At the beginning they started to earn money by doing acrobating shows in the streets of Florencia (Italia). They were so talented that a rich entrepreneur, called Donaldo Trumpetta, offered them the opportunity to find other artists, animals and invested to create a circus. The success story started there.
Today, the Wild Circus company is doing tours all over the world for more than 100 years now, and the Zavetti's family is a big part of that success.
